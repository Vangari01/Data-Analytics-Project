#Amazon Sales Analysis  Dashboard using SQL and Power BI
Analysed sales data using SQL to identify revenue trends and customer behaviour
Built interactive dashboards using Power BI to visualize catergory wise revenue, sales performance, payment methods used and discount offered.

Findings: 
1. Asia shows higher sales in the Books category, with strong usage of UPI as a payment method.
2. Europe demonstrates higher purchases in Fashion, primarily using Debit Cards and Wallets.
3. Middle East customers prefer Books, with Cash on Delivery (COD) being the top payment method.
4. North America leads in Beauty product sales, with Wallet and UPI usage.
5. Discounts were applied uniformly across all regions, with no region-specific targeting.

Recommendations:
1. Implement region-specific discount strategies instead of uniform discounts to maximize sales.
2. Offer targeted promotions based on payment methods:
   a. Cashback or incentives for UPI / Wallet users in Asia and North America
   b. Discounts or coupons for Debit Card users in Europe
   c. Special COD-based offers in the Middle East
